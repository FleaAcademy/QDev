### Category of Sets

Let $\textbf{Set}$ be the **category of sets** consisted by sets as **dots** and functions as **arrows**.

Le $X,Y \in \textbf{Set}$ be sets and $f \in \textbf{Set}$ be a function:

$$
\\begin{align}
f: X &\\to Y \\\\
x &\\mapsto y = f(x)
\\end{align}
$$
